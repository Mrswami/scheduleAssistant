# 🏛️ YMCA IT Internship: First Contact Success Checklist
**Objective:** Secure the IT/Data Internship by demonstrating that you are a high-level solution architect, not just an applicant.

---

### 1. 🎓 The "Education" Narrative (John's 3 Questions)
Since John asked for your program, school, and date, have your script ready:
- [ ] **The School:** UT Austin (Digital Media Arts).
- [ ] **The "Program":** Independent Full-Stack Residency / Advanced Software Engineering.
- [ ] **The Graduation/Status:** Focus on being "Market-Ready." *Tell him: "I've pivoted my focus from my initial Digital Media tenure to full-stack YMCA system architecture to maximize my utility to the Association Hub IT team."*
- [ ] **The Gain:** Goal is to master **Enterprise Data Synchronization** and assist in the **SocialSchedules ➡️ W2W Migration.**

---

### 2. 📱 The "Prototype" Prep (The Demo)
John has the link, but he might not have opened it yet. Prepare his "Tour":
- [ ] **Device Sync:** Ensure your phone (even with the cracked screen) is charged. If you meet virtually, have the Chrome browser open to `https://scheduleassistant-735d8.web.app/prototype/`.
- [ ] **The "Wow" Hook:** "While I was at the front desk, I identify a sync-gap in our W2W transition. So I built this **Real-Time Data Bridge** to solve it."
- [ ] **Technical Specs:** Be ready to mention your stack: **Flutter (Frontend)**, **Node.js/Express (Backend)**, **Firebase (Authentication/Cloud Messaging).**

---

### 3. 🧠 Smart Questions for John (Reverse Interview)
Directors love being asked about their "Pain Points." Ask him:
- [ ] *"What is the biggest data-quality hurdle the Hub is facing with the W2W move?"*
- [ ] *"What database architecture are we using for our Association-wide staff data? (SQL, Oracle, or something proprietary?)"*
- [ ] *"How can my prototype's API logic support our current IT security standards?"*
